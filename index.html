Gameplay

  1. Click in the large blue square to earn money, you will earn 1 gold per click
  2. Once you have 25 coins the Upgrade button will appear, click it and you will use 25 gold, but will earn one extra gold per click.
  3. Each time you buy an upgrade the cost of the next one will double.

The Shop

The Shop is where you can buy upgrades to help you earn more money, or change the appearance of your screen.

  1. The "Pointless" button does nothing because i'm to lazy to think of something to put there.
  2. The "+3" button increases he amount you earn by 3 per click, which i guess makes the upgrade button useless...
  3. The "Shiny Mouse" button gives you a loading mouse because the CSS was being dumb and wouldn't let me import a custom mouse D:

    4. When You Reach 2000 gold a "Win Game" button will appear, click it to reach the end game screen! (Don't worry, you can still play after)
